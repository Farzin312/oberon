# 11 — Portugal Wildfire 2024

## Location
Central Portugal, near Coimbra. ~40.12N, -8.27W. Mediterranean pine/eucalyptus.

## Change description
2024 wildfire season burn scar. Large areas of canopy consumed.
Sharp NDVI drop from fire, distinct from gradual deforestation.

## Source of truth
- EFFIS (European Forest Fire Information System) burnt area
- Copernicus Emergency Management Service
- Portuguese Nature and Forest Conservation Service (ICNF)

## Expected behavior
1-4 findings. Large NDVI drop (-0.20 to -0.50).
Burn scars have characteristic spectral signature (high NBR change).
Fire is a legitimate "disturbance" for this pipeline.
