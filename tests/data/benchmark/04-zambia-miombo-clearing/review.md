# 04 — Zambia Miombo Clearing

## Location
Central Province, Zambia. ~-14.97S, 26.52E. Miombo woodland belt.

## Change description
Smallholder shifting agriculture clearing in miombo woodland.
Scattered small clearings rather than industrial-scale.

## Source of truth
- Global Forest Watch
- IIED miombo research
- Sentinel-2 visual confirmation

## Expected behavior
1-5 findings. Moderate NDVI drop (-0.10 to -0.40).
Savanna has lower baseline NDVI than rainforest, so deltas are smaller.
Dry season imagery preferred for clarity.
