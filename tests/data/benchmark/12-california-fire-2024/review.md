# 12 — California Wildfire 2024

## Location
Santa Barbara County, California, USA. ~34.72N, -119.97W. Chaparral/mixed conifer.

## Change description
2024 California wildfire burn scar. Fast-moving fire through dry vegetation.
Sharp spectral change, especially in NBR.

## Source of truth
- CAL FIRE (California Department of Forestry and Fire Protection) perimeters
- MTBS (Monitoring Trends in Burn Severity)
- Sentinel-2 visual confirmation

## Expected behavior
1-4 findings. Large NDVI drop (-0.20 to -0.60).
California fires burn intensely — spectral change should be unambiguous.
May have cloud-free conditions in both windows (dry Mediterranean climate).
