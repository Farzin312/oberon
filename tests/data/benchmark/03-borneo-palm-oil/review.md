# 03 — Borneo Palm Oil Expansion

## Location
East Kalimantan, Indonesian Borneo. ~-0.77S, 113.52E.

## Change description
Conversion of tropical forest to oil palm plantation.
Regular geometric clearing pattern characteristic of industrial plantations.
